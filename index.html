Any Physicians with MBBS with valid MCI registration or final year MBBS students are eligible to join all programs. However, some certificate programs are available to Ayush and BDS graduates as well; please refer to icons and eligibility. All international students are eligible for certificate and advance certificate programs.

Get in touch with us at support@globalmedacademy.com or click at join now icon at the webpage and complete the payment process.

GlobalMed Academy offers self-paced topics and live lectures via our learning management system. Login to LMS and you would be guided through the process.

At GlobalMed Academy, clinical observership is part of the fellowship . This program helps students to gain experience in a hospital setting and perform a variety of tasks, such as being part of the decision-making process for patient care, as well as learning how to utilize equipment and observe experienced doctors performing procedures.

There would be online quiz post each segment. This would be done via LMS and once passing marks are secured in quiz, students can move to next level. Here are key assesments.

1.Online quiz after each segment.
2.Online exam after completing all modules.
3.If there is requirement of program director, there would be viva-voce.
4.For Fellowship students, All quizzes, online exam and Viva must be completed before observership.
5.Some hospitals may conduct its own exam after completion of observership. Student must share the certificate of completion of observership once its completed.
6.After completion of all the steps GlobalMed Academy would award certificate.
7.For each online exam there would be three attempts without any charges. However if there is need for further attempts then there would be examination fees applicable.
8.There is no fix schedule required for each online exam. Students may complete as per their schedule.
9.For viva-voice there would be schedule assigned and students must clear in first attempt. Next attempt would be chargeable and dates would be based on the availability of instructors

There are multiple accreditations available for each course and they are mentioned in brochure.
GlobalMed Academy does not offer any certificate using which a healthcare professional can call himself/herself specialist (i.e. diabetologist, cardiologist etc). However it imparts clinical knowledge and global trend about the specific topic so that student is able to give better care to the patients. In any way this program does not replace formal medical education, however it supports continuous learning of health care professionals.
None of the programme is recognized by Medical Council of India or any other state medical council.

Our courses can be accessed with Windows 7.0 or above or Mac OS X10.15 and above in any browser with minimum 1 GB RAM. However for best experience we recommend at least 10 MBPS internet connection and 2GB RAM.

Electronic certificate with enrolment ID will be sent to registered e-mail address within 24 hours of completion of course. We also send framed certificate to physical address, if required.

GlobalMed Academy does not provide any placement guarantee however our partner hospitals continuously need qualified professionals and those jobs would be posted on the LMS and we would be able to connect with right hospital

Yes, courses can be cancelled as per the refund policy. GlobalMed Academy offers 14 days no question asked refund policy for the courses un-touched (i.e. no login or access to any course). Please refer to detailed terms and condition page for more details.
